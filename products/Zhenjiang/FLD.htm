It is used to form a complete set of model BZZ steering control units. Under such conditions of variety oil volume or /and different pressure in steering system, model FLD can provide constant flow for the steering unit so as to meet the requirement of hydraulic steering performance of vehicle. It can not only control steering system, but also help pump to divide flow so that to reduce cost and simplify system design.
