E series air treatment units

We've manufactured E series air treatment units for 15 years with zero quality complaint and feedback. In current product lines, the E series air units are similar to SMC A series air preparation units and are our most popular style air units with higher flow rates, simple assembly and maintenance reduces down time. E series air treatment units are available for 8 sizes options from M5 to 1” to ensure optimum performance,offering energy savings. We’ve already finished R&D for the combination of modular style air preparation equipment that best suit your needs. With standard 25 micron avalable and 5micron as opetional, E series air treatment units are a good replacement for SMC air units.
